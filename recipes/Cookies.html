Chocolate Chip Cookies

This recipe will breakdown everything you need to know about baking fresh and warm chocolate chip cookies!

A chocolate chip cookies is a sweet baked treat that is recognized by its butter flavor and the inclusion of chocolate chips. Some variations can include nuts, oatmeal or rasins.

Commerically available formats of chocolate chips cookies include fresh, packaged, and frozen dough. Common variations also include Chewy, chrispy, and chunky!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts
